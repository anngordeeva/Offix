Our process

Our approach blends innovation, expertise, and client-centricity, shaping unparalleled achievements.

  • STEP 1

    Choose from Our Approved Business Centers

    We partner with licensed business centers across Dubai that are authorized by DED and RERA to issue Ejari certificates for virtual offices. You simply select the location that fits your needs — no need to search or negotiate.

  • STEP 2

    Sign the Lease Agreement

    Once you choose the location, we prepare and send you a 12-month tenancy contract. The agreement includes a registered office unit number suitable for Ejari.

  • STEP 3

    Ejari Registration

    We handle the Ejari registration process on your behalf through the official RERA system. No need for you to visit any government office.

  • STEP 4

    Payment of Fees

    You only pay once — no hidden charges. The full package includes:

    • Business center fees
    • Ejari registration
    • Full support and documentation
